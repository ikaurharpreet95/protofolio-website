📋 Design Process

A collaborative, four-phase process ensuring your vision becomes reality.

Phase 1: Discovery (Week 1)

  • Initial consultation call
  • Understanding client needs and vision
  • Event details collection
  • Style preference exploration
  • Budget and timeline confirmation
  • Mood board creation

Phase 2: Concept Development (Week 2)

  • Initial design concepts (2-3 options)
  • Color palette presentation
  • Typography selections
  • Layout options
  • Client feedback incorporation

Phase 3: Design Refinement (Week 2-3)

  • Detailed revisions based on feedback
  • Fine-tuning colors and elements
  • Additional iteration rounds
  • Finalized design approval
  • Print-ready file preparation

Phase 4: Finalization (Week 3)

  • Final artwork delivery
  • Print specifications
  • Color profile management
  • Printer recommendations
  • Final adjustments if needed
Typical Timeline: 2-4 weeks depending on design complexity and revision rounds.
