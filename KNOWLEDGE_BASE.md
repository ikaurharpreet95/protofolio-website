## 📋 FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: How long does the design process take?**
A: Typically 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Rush orders available with additional fee.

**Q: Can I get revisions?**
A: Yes! Each package includes revision rounds. Premium and Luxury packages have more flexibility.

**Q: Do you offer printing services?**
A: We coordinate with quality printers and provide print specifications, but don't print directly.

**Q: What if I don't know what I want?**
A: Perfect! That's what the consultation is for. Harpreet will guide you through discovering your style.

**Q: Are digital invitations really okay for weddings?**
A: Absolutely! Modern couples use digital invitations for eco-friendly options or as complements to printed ones.

**Q: What's the rush fee?**
A: Rush projects (1-2 weeks) incur an additional 20-30% fee depending on timeline.

**Q: Do you do revisions for free?**
A: Included revision rounds vary by package. Additional revisions are billed at hourly rate ($50/hour).

**Q: Can you work with my budget?**
A: Let's discuss! Harpreet offers solutions for various budgets. Book a consultation.
